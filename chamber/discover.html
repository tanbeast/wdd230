Discover Carey

Welcome to Discover Carey! Nestled in the heart of Idaho, Carey is a charming town brimming with natural beauty, vibrant culture, and warm hospitality. Whether you're seeking outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, or simply a relaxing getaway, Carey has something for everyone.

Explore the breathtaking landscapes surrounding Carey, from the rugged beauty of Craters of the Moon National Monument to the serene tranquility of our community park. Hike scenic trails, marvel at ancient lava flows, or simply unwind amidst the lush greenery of our town's parks and gardens.

Immerse yourself in Carey's rich cultural scene by attending the annual Renaissance Festival, where you can experience live performances, artisan crafts, and delectable cuisine. Don't miss our weekly sports events, where locals and visitors alike come together to enjoy friendly competition and camaraderie.

After a day of exploration, unwind in one of Carey's cozy cafes or dine al fresco under the stars at one of our charming restaurants. Whether you're craving comfort food or gourmet delights, our local eateries offer a variety of culinary delights to satisfy every palate.

Experience the magic of Carey and create unforgettable memories in our vibrant community.
